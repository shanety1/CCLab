About Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is an American Singer-Songwriter and often writes her song through inspirations of her personal life. She was born on December 13, 1989 in West Reading, Pennsylvania, and relocated to Nashville, Tennessee, at the age of 14 to pursue a career in country music.She signed a songwriting deal with Sony/ATV Music Publishing in 2004 and a recording deal with Big Machine Records in 2005, and released her eponymous debut studio album in 2006. As she continued her msuic, her fame continued to grow, releasing countless hit singles and albums
